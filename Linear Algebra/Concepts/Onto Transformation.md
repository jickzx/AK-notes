# Onto Transformation

## Definition

A transformation $T:\mathbb{R}^n\to\mathbb{R}^m$ is [[Onto Transformation|onto]] if every vector in the codomain is an output of $T$.

Equivalently, for every $\mathbf{b}\in\mathbb{R}^m$, the equation

$$
T(\mathbf{x})=\mathbf{b}
$$

has at least one solution.

## Why It Matters

For a matrix transformation $T(\mathbf{x})=A\mathbf{x}$, onto means:

- $A\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}$ is [[Consistent System|consistent]] for every $\mathbf{b}$
- the columns of $A$ span the codomain
- $A$ has a [[Pivot|pivot]] in every row
