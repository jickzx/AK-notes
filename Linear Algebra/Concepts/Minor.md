# Minor

## Definition

The $(i,j)$-[[Minor|minor]] of a matrix $A$ is the determinant of the matrix obtained by deleting row $i$ and column $j$.

It is usually written $M_{ij}$.

## Why It Matters

Minors are the building blocks for [[Cofactor|cofactors]] and cofactor expansion formulas for determinants.

## Example

If

$$
A=
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 0\\
0 & 3 & 4\\
5 & 0 & 6
\end{pmatrix},
$$

then

$$
M_{12}=
\begin{vmatrix}
0 & 4\\
5 & 6
\end{vmatrix}
=-20.
$$
