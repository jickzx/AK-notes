# Rank

## Definition

The [[Rank|rank]] of a matrix $A$ is the dimension of its [[Column Space|column space]]:

$$
\operatorname{rank}(A)=\dim(\operatorname{Col}(A)).
$$

Equivalently, rank is the number of [[Pivot|pivot]] columns.

## Why It Matters

Rank measures how many independent output directions a matrix has.

If $A$ has rank $2$, then its columns span a $2$-dimensional subspace.
