# Orthogonal Complement

## Definition

For a subspace $W$,

$$
W^\perp=\{\mathbf{x}\mid \mathbf{x}\cdot \mathbf{w}=0 \text{ for all } \mathbf{w}\in W\}.
$$

## Why It Matters

Orthogonal complements connect row spaces, null spaces, and orthogonal decompositions.
