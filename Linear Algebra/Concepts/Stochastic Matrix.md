# Stochastic Matrix

## Definition

A [[Stochastic Matrix|stochastic matrix]] has nonnegative entries and columns that sum to $1$.

## Why It Matters

It models transitions in probability systems and is used to study [[Steady State|steady states]].

## Example

The matrix

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
0.75 & 0.25\\
0.25 & 0.75
\end{pmatrix}
$$

is stochastic because all entries are nonnegative and each column sums to $1$.
