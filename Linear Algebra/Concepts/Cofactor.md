# Cofactor

## Definition

The $(i,j)$-[[Cofactor|cofactor]] of a matrix is

$$
C_{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M_{ij},
$$

where $M_{ij}$ is the corresponding [[Minor|minor]].

## Why It Matters

Cofactors are used in cofactor expansions, adjugate formulas, and Cramer's rule.

## Example

If $M_{12}=-20$, then

$$
C_{12}=(-1)^{1+2}(-20)=20.
$$
