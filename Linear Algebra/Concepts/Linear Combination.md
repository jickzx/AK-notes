# Linear Combination

## Definition

A linear combination is an expression of the form

$$
c_1\mathbf{v}_1 + c_2\mathbf{v}_2 + \cdots + c_k\mathbf{v}_k.
$$

## Why It Matters

Linear combinations generate [[Span|spans]] and appear naturally in [[Matrix Equation|matrix equations]].
