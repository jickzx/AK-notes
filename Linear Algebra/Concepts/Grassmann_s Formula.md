# Grassmann's Formula

## Statement

If $U$ and $V$ are [[Subspace|subspaces]] of $\mathbb{R}^n$, then

$$
\dim(U)+\dim(V)=\dim(U+V)+\dim(U\cap V).
$$

## Why It Matters

This formula explains how dimensions combine when two subspaces overlap.

The dimension of the intersection is the amount of double-counting that must be subtracted.
