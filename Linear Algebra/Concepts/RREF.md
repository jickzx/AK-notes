# RREF

## Definition

RREF stands for reduced row echelon form. In RREF, every [[Pivot|pivot]] is equal to $1$ and is the only nonzero entry in its column.

## Why It Matters

RREF makes it easiest to read off solutions, contradictions, and [[Free Variable|free variables]].
