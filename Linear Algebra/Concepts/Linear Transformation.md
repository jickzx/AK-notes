# Linear Transformation

## Definition

A transformation

$$
T:\mathbb{R}^n\to\mathbb{R}^m
$$

is [[Linear Transformation|linear]] if

$$
T(\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v})=T(\mathbf{u})+T(\mathbf{v})
$$

and

$$
T(c\mathbf{u})=cT(\mathbf{u})
$$

for all vectors $\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v}$ and scalars $c$.

## Why It Matters

Linear transformations are exactly the transformations that can be represented by matrices.

So they connect abstract vector-space ideas to concrete matrix computations.
