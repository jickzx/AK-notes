# Range

## Definition

The [[Range|range]] of a transformation is the set of all actual outputs.

For a matrix transformation

$$
T(\mathbf{x})=A\mathbf{x},
$$

the range is the [[Column Space|column space]] of $A$.

## Why It Matters

The transformation is [[Onto Transformation|onto]] exactly when its range equals its [[Codomain|codomain]].
