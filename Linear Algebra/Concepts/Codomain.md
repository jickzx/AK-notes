# Codomain

## Definition

The [[Codomain|codomain]] of a transformation is the space where outputs are supposed to live.

For a matrix transformation

$$
T:\mathbb{R}^n\to\mathbb{R}^m,
$$

the codomain is $\mathbb{R}^m$.
