# Logical Symbols

**Conjunction (AND): **P ∧ Q

**Disjunction (OR): **P ∨ Q

**Negation (NOT): **¬P or P̅

**Implication (IF...THEN): **P → Q

**Biconditional (IF AND ONLY IF): **P ↔ Q

**Exclusive OR (XOR): **P ⊕ Q

**Tautology (Always True): **⊤

**Contradiction (Always False): **⊥

**Entailment (Logical Consequence): **P ⊨ Q

**Logical Equivalence: **P ≡ Q

# Set Theory & Predicate Symbols

Universal Quantifier: ∀

There exists in a set: ∃

Element of a set: ∈

Subset of a set: ⊆

Proper Subset of a set: ⊂
